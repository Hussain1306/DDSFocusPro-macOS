Getting Started

To start tracking your work time:

  1. Select a Project from the dropdown menu
  2. Choose a Task associated with that project
  3. Make sure you're in Work Mode (the button should be highlighted)
  4. Click the START button
  5. The timer will begin counting, and screen recording will automatically start

When you click the Finish button:

  • A modal will appear asking you to describe what you completed
  • Enter detailed notes about your work
  • Click Submit to save your time entry
  • Your timesheet will be automatically sent to the system
  • Screenshots taken during the session will be uploaded

Work Mode

Work Mode is the primary mode for tracking regular work sessions. When in Work Mode:

  • The timer tracks time spent on selected tasks
  • Screen recording is active (based on your screenshot interval)
  • Activity logs are captured automatically
  • Idle detection is enabled to pause the timer when you're away

No, you cannot switch tasks while the timer is running. You must:

  1. Click Finish to complete the current task
  2. Enter your task completion notes
  3. Select a new project and task
  4. Click START again to begin tracking the new task

This ensures accurate time tracking for each task.

You must work for at least 1 minute before you can finish a task. If you try to finish before 1 minute has elapsed, you'll see a warning message.

Meeting Mode

To track a meeting:

  1. Click the Meeting Mode button
  2. Select the project and task related to the meeting
  3. Click START to begin tracking
  4. When the meeting ends, click Finish
  5. Enter meeting notes describing what was discussed
  6. Click Submit to save

Yes! If you're in Work Mode and need to attend a meeting:

  • Switch to Meeting Mode - your work timer will pause
  • Track the meeting time
  • When finished, enter meeting notes
  • Switch back to Work Mode to resume your work timer
  • Both work and meeting notes will be saved when you finish the work session

Screenshots & Recording

Screen recording automatically starts when you begin tracking time:

  • Screenshots are captured at intervals set by your administrator
  • The interval is shown in your dashboard (e.g., \"5 minutes\")
  • Screenshots are automatically uploaded when you finish a task
  • All screenshots are securely stored and accessible only to authorized personnel

Yes! Your screenshot interval is displayed on the main dashboard under the \"Screenshot Interval\" label. This setting is configured by your system administrator.

The system has idle detection to ensure accurate time tracking:

  • If you're inactive for 3 minutes, the system detects you as idle
  • The timer automatically pauses
  • A modal notification appears
  • After 5 seconds, your work is auto-saved with adjusted time (excluding idle time)
  • This ensures you're only paid for active work time

Important Notes

Please ensure your internet connection is stable and working properly before starting work.

  • A good internet connection is required for screenshots to upload successfully
  • Time logs and activity data need connectivity to sync with the server
  • Check your connection status before clicking START
  • If you experience connection issues during work, your data may not be saved properly
  • Always verify that your submissions are successful before logging out

Always properly close your work or meeting mode after finishing tasks.

  • Click the Finish button to complete your session
  • Enter your task completion notes or meeting details
  • Click Submit and wait for confirmation
  • Do NOT close the browser or application until submission is complete
  • Incomplete sessions may result in lost time tracking data
  • Ensure you see a success message before moving to another task

Failure to properly close sessions can lead to:

  • Lost screenshots and activity logs
  • Incomplete timesheet entries
  • Inaccurate work hour calculations

Troubleshooting

If you can't select a task, try these steps:

  1. Make sure you've selected a Project first
  2. Wait a moment for tasks to load (you'll see "Loading tasks...")
  3. If no tasks appear, the project may not have any tasks assigned
  4. Contact your project manager if tasks should be available

The START button is disabled when:

  • A timer is already running (work or meeting)
  • You haven't selected both a project and task
  • You need to finish your current session first

Complete or finish your current activity before starting a new one.

No, you cannot log out while a timer is active. This prevents accidental data loss. You must:

  1. Click Finish to complete your task/meeting
  2. Submit your notes
  3. Then you can safely log out

The logout button will be disabled and grayed out when a timer is running.

If you experience syncing issues:

  1. Check your internet connection immediately
  2. Make sure you have a stable internet connection before starting work
  3. Try refreshing the page
  4. Log out and log back in
  5. Clear your browser cache
  6. If the issue persists, contact IT support

⚠️ Important: Always ensure a good internet connection is available before starting any work session. Poor connectivity can result in lost screenshots, incomplete uploads, and missing timesheet data.
